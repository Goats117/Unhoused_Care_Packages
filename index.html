Caring For The Community,
One Package at a Time

Our goal is to create care packages for those who are unhoused to help overcome daily challenges

What's in the Packages?

It changes seasonally to help with certain challenges. In the Winter, we include items like hand warmers and gloves. In the summer, maybe a ball cap and some Sunscreen. We also include food and personal care products, like nail clippers.
